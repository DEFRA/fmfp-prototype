Not a flood risk expert? Find out what this means, and what you should do next.

 

To request flood risk assessment data (previously known as a product 4) you’ll need an email address we can send the information to.

You’ll receive the flood risk assessment data as a PDF file within 2 hours.

Depending on the data that's available, this will include:

  • a flood zone map (flood map for planning)
  • historic flood outlines
  • relevant modelled flood levels and extents
  • flood defence locations and attributes
