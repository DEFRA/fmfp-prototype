Refine your search

To refine your search you can either:

  • move the marker on the map to identify a specific location.
  • draw a shape on the map to mark out a site boundary.
How to draw a shape
  • select 'Draw shape'
  • select a second point on the boundary - you will see a line drawn between the first two points
  • keep adding points until the boundary is defined
  • double click or tap when you return to your first point
  • edit the finished shape by dragging the points
  • add more points to a shape by clicking or tapping on a line
