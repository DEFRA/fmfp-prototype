Get flood risk information for planning

Only use this service for flood risk for planning purposes. It only covers flood risk from rivers and sea.

For local flood warnings, use the flood information service.

For general information on your long term flood risk including risk from surface water use the check your long term flood risk service.

Use this service to:

  • find out if you need a flood risk assessment as part of a planning application (sometimes known as planning permission)
  • get a flood map for planning purposes showing your flood zone
  • request a detailed flood risk assessment map (previously known as product 4)
  • find contact details to request:
    • flood modelling and hydrology reports and modelling guidelines
    • model output data,
    • calibrated and verified model input data
    • flood defence breach hazard map

Before you start

You will need the postcode, place, national grid reference (NGR) or easting and northing co-ordinates for the location you want to know about.

You’ll usually need a flood risk consultant to carry out a flood risk assessment. If it’s for a simple, low risk development like a house extension you may be able to do it yourself.

If you’re unsure contact the Environment Agency.
