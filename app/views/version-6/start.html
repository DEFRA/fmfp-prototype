Get flood risk information for planning in England

Use this service for flood risk information for planning purposes. It only covers flood risk from rivers and sea.

For local flood warnings, use the flood information service.

For general information on your long term flood risk including risk from surface water use the check your long term flood risk service.

Get flood risk information for Wales, Scotland or Northern Ireland.

Use this service to:

  • find out if you need a flood risk assessment as part of a planning application (sometimes known as planning permission)
  • get a flood map for planning showing your flood zone
  • request detailed flood risk data needed to carry out a flood risk assessment (previously known as product 4)
  • find contact details to request:
    • flood modelling and hydrology reports and modelling guidelines
    • model output data,
    • calibrated and verified model input data
    • flood defence breach hazard map

Before you start

For the location you want to know about you will need (one of the following):

  • postcode
  • place
  • national grid reference (NGR)
  • easting and northing coordinates
