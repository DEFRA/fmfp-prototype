Map products and how to get them

Map products are packages of information that you need to carry out a flood risk assessment.

The map product you need for your FRA depends on:

  • the flood zone it is in
  • the size of the development
  • If it is behind flood defences

You can get the map products you need from this page:

  • Map product 1 - Download the map (PDF)
  • Map product 4 - Request a product 4
  • Map products 5,6,7 and 8 - Contact the Environment Agency

Map products 5,6,7 and 8

To request map products 5,6,7 or 8 contact the Environment Agency (EA).

A local EA team will deal with your request. To ensure you receive the correct data you will need to provide one of the following:

  • a map showing the site boundary, with either a grid reference or postcode
  • an aerial photo with a grid reference
  • a GIS (Geographical Information System) polygon file of the boundary
